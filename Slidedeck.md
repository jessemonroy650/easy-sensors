##What is IoT?##

Sensor, attached to computer, attached to Internet.

The five Senses --> Sight, Sound, Smell, Touch, Taste --> 4 of 5 make IoT

Sensors can detect - Light, Vision, Sound, Smell, Temperature, Tilt, Vibration, and more.
